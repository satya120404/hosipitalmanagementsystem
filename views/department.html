Hospital Departments

Cardiology Department

Our Cardiology Department provides comprehensive care for all kinds of heart-related conditions. Our team of expert cardiologists uses the latest techniques and equipment to diagnose and treat patients with heart diseases.

Neurology Department

The Neurology Department focuses on diagnosing and treating disorders of the nervous system. We provide treatment for conditions such as stroke, epilepsy, migraines, and neurological injuries.

Pediatrics Department

Our Pediatrics Department offers healthcare services for infants, children, and adolescents. We provide routine check-ups, vaccinations, and treatment for various pediatric illnesses.

Orthopedic Department

The Orthopedic Department specializes in diagnosing and treating musculoskeletal conditions. Our orthopedic surgeons are experienced in joint replacements, spinal surgeries, and other orthopedic procedures.
